Société des Nocturnes

"Per noctes eorum vagemur ut umbrae"

Founded in the midnight shadow of a hangman's tree, the Société des Nocturnes gathers discreetly to explore our lost, atrophied arcane senses: remote viewing, dream communion, momentary prophecy, and the tracking of synchronicities. We do not claim gifts, only practice.

The Society convenes across unseen channels. Most members have never met. All have felt the pull. If you have dreamt of a place before visiting it, if you have spoken someone's thought aloud, or if the universe occasionally winks at you — you may already belong.

Membership is not offered. It is recognized. If you suspect you are one of us, you are invited to send a signal. The Society does not recruit. It listens.
